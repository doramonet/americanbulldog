About American Bulldogs

The American Bulldog is a powerful and loyal breed known for its strength and affectionate nature. They are versatile dogs that can be both great family pets and effective working dogs. Known for their muscular build and energetic personality, American Bulldogs are intelligent and protective.

Key Characteristics

  • Breed Group: Working
  • Size: Large
  • Coat: Short, smooth
  • Color: Various, including white, brindle, and brown
  • Temperament: Loyal, courageous, friendly
